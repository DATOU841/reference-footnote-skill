# Claim Taxonomy

Claim types:

- `factual_claim`
- `theoretical_claim`
- `historical_narrative`
- `data_judgment`
- `policy_judgment`
- `academic_judgment`
- `author_opinion`
- `logical_inference`
- `definition`
- `common_knowledge`
- `transitional`

`author_opinion`, `common_knowledge`, and `transitional` are protected from forced citation.
